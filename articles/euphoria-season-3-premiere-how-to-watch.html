'Euphoria' Season 3 Premiere Date, Time, and How to Watch

The wait is finally over. HBO's Emmy-winning drama 'Euphoria' returns for its highly anticipated third season, bringing fans back into the complex world of Rue and her friends.

Set five years after the tumultuous events of Season 2, the new installment finds the characters navigating their 20s with a "grown-up" look. Production delays, including the 2023 Hollywood strikes, have only heightened the anticipation for what Sam Levinson has teased as a potential "slam dunk" finale for the series.

Premiere Date and Time

'Euphoria' Season 3 premieres on Sunday, April 12, 2026, at 9 p.m. ET/PT.

The show will air specifically on HBO and will be available for simultaneous streaming on HBO Max. Following the premiere, new episodes will drop weekly every Sunday through May 31.

Where to Stream 'Euphoria' Season 3

For those looking to watch online, HBO Max is the exclusive streaming home for 'Euphoria'. You can access the premiere and all subsequent episodes via the app or web portal at the scheduled air time.

Full Episode Schedule

Season 3 consists of 8 episodes. Here is the scheduled release calendar:

  • Episode 1: April 12
  • Episode 2: April 19
  • Episode 3: April 26
  • Episode 4: May 3
  • Episode 5: May 10
  • Episode 6: May 17
  • Episode 7: May 24
  • Episode 8: May 31 (Season Finale)

The Season 3 Cast

The returning ensemble features some of the biggest names in Hollywood today:

  • Zendaya as Rue
  • Hunter Schafer as Jules Vaughn
  • Jacob Elordi as Nate Jacobs
  • Sydney Sweeney as Cassie Howard
  • Alexa Demie as Maddy
  • Maude Apatow as Lexi Howard
  • Eric Dane as Cal Jacobs
  • Martha Kelly as Laurie
  • Colman Domingo as Ali Muhammed

New additions to the cast include Adewale Akinnuoye-Agbaje and Toby Wallace, adding fresh dynamics to the five-year jump narrative.

Is this the Final Season?

While HBO Max has not officially confirmed a Season 4, showrunner Sam Levinson and star Zendaya have hinted that closure is coming. Levinson stated he wrote this season with the focus of delivering a definitive end, while Zendaya recently shared on 'The Drew Barrymore Show' that Closure is indeed on the horizon for these characters.

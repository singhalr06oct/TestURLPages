Justin Bieber's History-Making Coachella Set Backed by Hailey Bieber, Tems, and Wizkid

Justin Bieber took the main stage at the 2026 Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival to deliver a history-making performance that has left fans around the globe speechless. Behind the scenes and from the crowd, his wife Hailey Bieber hyped up the superstar setting a high watermark for couple goals in the music industry.

During his highly anticipated festival set, Justin proved he is still a monumental force in modern pop music. Delivering unparalleled showmanship, Bieber shocked the massive crowd by bringing Nigerian superstars Wizkid and Tems out to the stage.
